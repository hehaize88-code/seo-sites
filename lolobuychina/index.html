FAQ

What does W2C mean?

W2C means Where To Cop. It is used when shoppers want the original source link or item page.

What does QC mean?

QC means Quality Check. In agent shopping, it usually refers to warehouse photos used before international shipping.

Are product cards local detail pages?

No. Cards open the matching source detail page directly so users do not pass through another duplicated local product layer.

Can I search categories?

Yes. Search shoes, hoodie, t-shirt, pants, accessories or product IDs to open the closest matching source page.

Is shipping price fixed?

No. Shipping depends on country, weight, volume, route and final checkout conditions.
