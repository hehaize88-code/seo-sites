LoloBuyChina FAQ for W2C Finds, QC Photos and Shipping Preparation

This FAQ gives LoloBuyChina a dedicated question page for search visitors who need clear answers before using product routes, spreadsheet finds or final product pages.

Independent guide note: This FAQ is informational. Always verify live product pages, platform terms, shipping rules and final costs before ordering.
What is LoloBuyChina?

LoloBuyChina is an independent product discovery and route guide site. It organizes W2C finds, product categories, QC photo reminders and shipping preparation notes.

Is LoloBuyChina an official shopping platform?

No. It is not an official platform page and does not process orders directly. It points visitors toward product-route research and buyer workflow checks.

What does W2C mean?

W2C means “where to cop.” In this context it refers to finding a product path, category route or live product destination that should be verified before ordering.

What should I check before opening a product route?

Check the category, product name, image, price estimate, size notes and whether the route looks consistent with the item you actually want.

What should I review in QC photos?

Check shape, color, material, stitching, print placement, tags, labels, hardware, accessories, packaging and obvious defects.

Are spreadsheet finds always reliable?

No. A spreadsheet is an organization tool. Buyers still need to check live pages, QC photos, item details and shipping rules.

Why are shipping estimates not fixed?

Shipping can depend on weight, volume, item type, route restrictions and current platform rules. Always verify current costs before shipping.

Can one parcel include every product type?

Not always. Some products may have route restrictions or special handling requirements, especially electronics, batteries, liquids or bulky packaging.

How can I reduce mistakes before shipment?

Use a checklist: product link, size, quantity, QC photos, packaging, route type, estimated weight and final decision.

Why does an independent FAQ help the site?

It gives search engines and visitors a clear page for recurring questions instead of hiding all answers inside the homepage.
