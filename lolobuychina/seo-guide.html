How LoloBuyChina visitors should read W2C finds

W2C search intent usually starts with a product idea, a screenshot, a category or a spreadsheet row. A useful route page should help the visitor understand the product category, visible image, product notes, price estimate and next step before opening a final product destination.

Use LoloBuyChina as a route hub, not as a shortcut around inspection. The safest workflow is to compare several products, keep notes, open the live destination, then wait for warehouse or platform-side confirmation before approving shipment.

QC photo review: what to inspect before shipping

Overall look: review silhouette, color, left-right symmetry and obvious flaws.
Details: check print placement, embroidery, stitching, labels, tags, hardware and small accessories.
Size notes: compare measurements, tag size, fit notes and seller descriptions.
Decision: approve only when product photos, notes and route expectations match closely enough.

Weight planning

Shoes, jackets and electronics can raise parcel cost. Keep an approximate weight note before combining items.

Volume planning

Boxes and bulky clothing may trigger volume-weight concerns. Check packaging before selecting a shipping path.

Route restrictions

Batteries, liquids, special materials and sensitive item categories may require different shipping routes.

Spreadsheet organization for LoloBuyChina

A practical spreadsheet should separate product title, category, live route, price estimate, QC notes, size note, shipping risk and final decision. This prevents visitors from treating every product card as equal. A spreadsheet is a review system, not a guarantee.
