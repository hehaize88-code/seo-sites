CSSBuy Store Product Shelf Guide 2026: W2C Cards, QC Photos and Shipping Prep

A store-style product shelf should help buyers move from browsing to a clear order decision. Product cards are helpful, but each card should connect to W2C notes, QC review and shipping preparation.

Product Shelf Context

Each shelf item should explain category, source link, visible product image and what needs to be checked. A shoe card should mention size and box concerns. A clothing card should remind buyers to confirm measurements. Accessories may require detail photos.

QC Photos After Arrival

Warehouse photos are the point where the buyer can compare the actual item with the source page. Review shape, colour, labels, stitching, accessories and packaging before shipping.

Shipping Preparation

Before submitting the parcel, review actual weight, volume weight, packaging choice and route restrictions. Product shelves should not promise a final cost before the item has been measured.

Buyer Notes

A useful note system tracks source link, item option, QC status, extra photo request and final shipping choice. This helps avoid repeated mistakes and unclear purchases.

Conclusion

CSSBuy Store works best when product cards are connected to inspection and parcel planning, not treated as final recommendations.
