QC Desk Before Shipping

Review product photos, shape, stitching, tags, color and sizing before shipment.

QC content helps buyers slow down before paying international shipping. A product route may be found, but warehouse photos decide whether the item is acceptable.

CSSBuy public guidance says quality inspection is carried out after warehouse arrival and photos are taken for users to view. The homepage also mentions unlimited QC images.

Many users ask about extra photos and video. I did not find a verified public extra-photo price or video-QC price on the reviewed pages, so a responsible article should not invent those costs.

A good QC checklist varies by item. Shoes need shape and sole checks. Hoodies need fabric, print and tag checks. Bags need hardware and lining checks. Electronics need package and surface-condition checks.

QC is connected to return timing. CSSBuy says returns can be helped when conditions are met, including item being in warehouse less than 7 days and return shipping fees being paid.

Good QC does not mean perfection. It is a reasonable decision based on product type, price, defect risk and buyer tolerance.

Independent-site disclaimer: this site does not take QC photos, sell extra-photo services, shoot videos or process returns.

FAQ: CSSBuy facts, W2C and QC

How much does CSSBuy shipping cost?

CSSBuy shipping is route-based. Public examples include USA HZ-FEDEX-F at CN¥235 for the first 500g plus CN¥55 per additional 500g, Brazil FJ-BR-EXP at CN¥50 first 100g plus CN¥10.5 per next 100g, and Europe YW-CNLine-F at CN¥35 first 100g plus CN¥6.5 per next 100g.

Is shipping price fixed?

No. Final cost depends on destination, route, actual weight, parcel volume, product attributes and current line restrictions.

What does the CSSBuy calculator check?

It asks for product weight, volume, commodity attributes and destination, then estimates available shipping methods, transit time and shipping fee.

What is volumetric weight?

Volumetric weight is chargeable weight based on parcel dimensions. Many freight lines charge by whichever is larger: actual weight or volume weight.

What does warehouse QC check?

Public CSSBuy guidance describes warehouse inspection and photos for user review. Buyers should check appearance, size, shape, color, tags and obvious defects.

Can CSSBuy help with returns?

CSSBuy says returns can be helped when return conditions are met, the item has been in warehouse less than 7 days and return shipping fees are paid.
