About hoobuys.net

Hoobuys.net is an independent research and navigation guide for people comparing product routes, W2C links, QC checks and parcel preparation.

✓ Independent guide✓ Direct source links✓ Dated updates

Our role

We publish explanatory guides, category routes and dated checks. Hoobuys.net is not a marketplace, does not accept orders or payments, and cannot access customer accounts, warehouse records or parcels.

What we check

Before publishing a route, we check whether the destination loads, whether the title and image fit the stated category, and whether the page gives users a useful next step. These checks do not authenticate products or guarantee future availability.

Information that changes

Prices, stock, exchange rates, coupons, service charges, shipping routes and warehouse options can change. The live destination page, account panel and checkout remain the final source for a transaction.
