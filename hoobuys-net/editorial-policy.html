Editorial policy

Our editorial process is designed to separate verified observations from estimates and to keep changing shopping information clearly qualified.

✓ Independent guide✓ Direct source links✓ Dated updates

Source hierarchy

We prefer current product and category pages, official service panels and the live checkout over old screenshots or copied price claims. When a fact can change, we name the final source users should check.

Verification method

We open destination links, compare the visible title, image and category, record a review date and remove routes that no longer match. QC guidance explains what a buyer can inspect; it is not an authenticity certificate.

Corrections and updates

Material updates receive a new review date. Expired links are replaced or removed, and fixed prices are avoided when destination, parcel weight, route or service settings affect the result.
