Complete Hoobuys FAQ

Answers about W2C, QC, photos, video, shipping, exchange rates, coupons and pre-shipment checks.

Frequently asked questions

How should a W2C route work?

Use category entrances, product routes and filters to answer where to buy, then provide a checklist for the final decision.

What should I check in QC photos?

Check the whole item first, then details: shoe shape and soles, clothing fabric and stitching, and accessory hardware and dimensions.

When should I request an extra photo?

Request another photo when images are blurred, a key area is missing, the size is unclear or a higher-value item needs confirmation.

When is a video useful?

Video is useful for moving, opening, lighting, ports, zips or details that are difficult to confirm in photos.

Why is there no fixed shipping price?

Destination, route, actual and volumetric weight, packaging and item type all affect the final price.

Where can I check exchange rates and coupons?

Use the account and checkout display because exchange rates, service fees and coupon rules can change.

How many free photos are included?

Check the warehouse service panel; the number can vary by order, warehouse status or platform rule.

How do I check the extra-photo price?

Use the price shown when requesting the photo and describe the exact area before confirming.

What matters most before ordering?

Confirm that the link works, colour and size are correct, notes are clear, images can be checked and a suitable route exists.

What should I check before shipping?

Confirm items, warehouse images, weight, route restrictions, insurance, recipient details and parcel consolidation.

What if a product link expires?

Do not order an unverified substitute. Return to the category route, filter again and check images and notes.

How can I reduce risk?

Start with a small order, inspect QC, avoid unclear information, confirm the route, then decide whether to consolidate.
