How to verify a product route

A product card is a starting point, not a guarantee that the same colour, size or version remains available. Open the detail page, compare the current images with the card and record the item ID before ordering.

  • Confirm that the product page still loads and matches the expected item.
  • Check colour, size, material notes and seller options.
  • Save the item ID and the date of the last check.
  • Review warehouse QC photos before approving international shipment.

If a link changes or expires, return to the category route and verify a new candidate instead of ordering an unreviewed substitute.
