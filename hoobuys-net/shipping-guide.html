Prepare the parcel before choosing a route

International shipping depends on destination, item type, actual weight, volumetric weight and packaging. Shoe boxes, thick coats and large lightweight items can cost more because the parcel occupies more space.

  • Compare actual and volumetric weight in the parcel estimate.
  • Check route restrictions for batteries, liquids, branded goods and other sensitive categories.
  • Decide whether boxes or excess packaging should be removed.
  • Confirm insurance, declaration, recipient details and delivery expectations.
  • Apply an eligible coupon only after the route and weight are known.

Use the live parcel submission and checkout screen for the final amount. A guide cannot promise a fixed shipping price because routes and calculations change.
