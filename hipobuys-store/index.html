FAQ for storefront visitors

Why were the first images missing?

The emergency homepage repair used letter placeholders. This update restores product thumbnails with real image sources.

Are product links still clickable?

Yes. Each thumbnail and route link opens the matching product page on the main product index.

What should I check in QC photos?

Look at item shape, color, stitching, labels, size tags, accessories and any defects before shipment.

Can I use this for W2C searches?

Yes. The page is organized around category and product-route intent so users can find a direction faster.
