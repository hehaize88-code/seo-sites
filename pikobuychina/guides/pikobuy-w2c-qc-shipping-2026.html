Pikobuy W2C Guide 2026: QC Photos, Spreadsheet Research and Shipping Workflow

Buying products from China requires more than finding a link. A reliable workflow combines product discovery, W2C research, QC photo review and shipping preparation.

Why Use a Pikobuy Spreadsheet?

A spreadsheet helps buyers organize products from different sources. Instead of saving random pages, users can record product names, categories, links, size information, QC notes and shipping considerations. This creates a repeatable research system.

For beginners, this method makes Chinese marketplace shopping easier because every product decision has supporting information. For experienced buyers, spreadsheets become a history record for future purchases.

Understanding W2C Product Routes

W2C means where to find a product. A useful W2C route should include enough context: what the item is, where it comes from, what details need checking and what possible risks exist.

Before moving forward, buyers should compare product information, images and available options. A link alone does not provide enough information for a confident decision.

How QC Photos Improve Decisions

QC photos are one of the most important steps before international shipping. They allow buyers to review visible details including shape, color, stitching, materials, labels and accessories.

Different products require different checks. Shoes should be reviewed for symmetry and construction. Clothing requires attention to measurements, fabric appearance and tags. Accessories require checking hardware and finishing.

Planning Shipping Before Final Submission

International shipping depends on many factors including destination, parcel weight, package volume and route selection. Good preparation means checking product details first and selecting shipping after QC approval.

A Better Buyer Workflow

A practical workflow is simple: discover products, save W2C information, review QC photos, confirm details, organize the parcel and complete shipping preparation.

The goal of a structured process is reducing mistakes and creating a smoother shopping experience.

Advanced Spreadsheet Tips

Experienced buyers can add extra notes such as previous purchase experience, sizing feedback, shipping performance and product comparisons. Over time, the spreadsheet becomes a personal shopping database.

Conclusion

Pikobuy research works best when buyers combine product discovery, W2C understanding, QC review and shipping planning. A clear workflow helps transform scattered information into organized purchasing decisions.
