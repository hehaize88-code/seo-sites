PikobuyChina Spreadsheet Guide 2026: W2C Finds, QC Photos, Parcel Notes and Shipping Decisions

A spreadsheet helps buyers turn scattered product finds into a structured workflow. PikobuyChina should guide visitors from W2C discovery to QC review, parcel planning and final shipping decisions.

Spreadsheet Columns That Matter

Useful columns include product name, category, W2C link, image reference, estimated price, size notes, QC status, extra photo request, parcel weight and final decision. These notes help buyers compare products instead of relying on memory.

W2C Finds Need Verification

A W2C find should not be treated as a guarantee. Open the source page, check visible options, review seller notes and confirm whether the product still appears available before submitting an order.

QC Photos and Product Approval

QC photos should be reviewed by category. Shoes need side, heel and sole checks. Clothing needs measurements and tags. Accessories need hardware and packaging confirmation. Missing angles should be recorded before approval.

Parcel and Shipping Notes

The final shipping decision depends on actual weight, volume weight, packaging and route availability. A spreadsheet can record estimated weight and route concerns before the final parcel is submitted.

Conclusion

PikobuyChina works best when it connects spreadsheet organization, W2C validation, QC photos and shipping decisions into one clear buyer workflow.
