Pikobuy Spreadsheet 2026 Guide: W2C Finds, QC Photos and Shipping Workflow

Pikobuy Spreadsheet is a structured way for buyers to organize China shopping research. Instead of saving random links, a useful spreadsheet connects products, W2C routes, QC photo checks and shipping preparation into one workflow.

Understanding Pikobuy W2C Finds

W2C means where to find a product. A good W2C entry should include product context, category, source information, images, size notes and possible risks. Buyers should always verify live product information before making decisions.

How Pikobuy Spreadsheet Helps Buyers

A spreadsheet makes comparison easier. Useful columns include product name, category, source link, estimated price, QC notes, size information, shipping concerns and final decision. This approach helps beginners understand products before moving forward.

Pikobuy QC Photos Checklist

QC photos are an important inspection step before international shipping. Buyers can review shape, color, stitching, materials, labels, accessories and packaging details. Good QC review reduces mistakes caused by unclear product images.

Shipping Preparation and Cost Planning

International shipping cost depends on destination, parcel weight, volume, route selection and packaging. Heavy items, shoe boxes and oversized packages may affect final cost. Buyers should compare shipping options before submitting a parcel.

Beginner Pikobuy Buying Workflow

Step one: discover products. Step two: check W2C information. Step three: review QC photos. Step four: confirm shipping options. Step five: prepare the final parcel. A simple process makes China shopping easier to understand.

Advanced Research Tips

Experienced buyers compare multiple sources, record QC history and maintain product notes. A well-organized Pikobuy spreadsheet becomes a long-term research tool rather than a simple product list.
