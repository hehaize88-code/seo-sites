Pikobuy W2C Finds

W2C means where to find a product. A useful product route should include category, images, source information and notes before a buyer makes a decision.

Pikobuy QC Photos Checklist

QC photos help review shape, color, stitching, materials, labels and packaging before shipping. Careful checking improves purchase confidence.

Pikobuy Shipping Guide

Shipping cost depends on weight, volume, destination, packaging and route selection. Buyers should review final parcel details before submission.

Spreadsheet Research Method

Track product name, W2C route, price estimate, QC notes and shipping decisions. A spreadsheet becomes a long-term research system.
