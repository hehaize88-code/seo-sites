Pikobuy W2C Finds

W2C means where to find a product. A useful route should include product information, images, category, notes and a clear research path. Buyers should verify current product details before making decisions.

Pikobuy QC Photos Checklist

QC photos help review shape, color, materials, stitching, labels, accessories and packaging before shipping. A careful inspection step improves buying confidence.

Shipping Workflow

Shipping costs are affected by destination, weight, volume, packaging and route choice. Planning before submitting a parcel helps avoid unexpected changes.

Spreadsheet Research Method

Track product name, source, price estimate, QC notes, size information and final decisions. A spreadsheet becomes a long-term shopping research system.
