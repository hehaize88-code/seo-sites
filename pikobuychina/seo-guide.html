Pikobuy Spreadsheet 2026 Guide: W2C Finds, QC Photos and Shipping Workflow

This complete guide explains how buyers research products, organize W2C links, review QC photos and prepare international shipping decisions.

What Is Pikobuy Spreadsheet 2026?

A Pikobuy Spreadsheet is a structured research system that helps buyers organize products, categories, source information and purchase notes. Instead of saving random links, buyers can compare products through a clearer workflow.

A useful spreadsheet is not only a list of products. It connects product discovery, W2C information, QC inspection and shipping preparation.

Pikobuy W2C Finds

W2C means where to find a product. A good W2C route should include product category, images, source details, size information and important notes.

Before making a decision, buyers should review current product information and compare available options. This reduces mistakes caused by incomplete links.

Pikobuy QC Photos Checklist

QC photos allow buyers to review visible details before shipment. Important points include shape, color, materials, stitching, labels, accessories and packaging.

For shoes, check symmetry and construction. For clothing, check fabric, tags and measurements. For accessories, review hardware and finishing details.

Pikobuy Shipping Guide

Shipping cost depends on destination, parcel weight, package volume, route selection and item characteristics.

Buyers should prepare shipping decisions after QC approval. Combining products may improve efficiency, but weight and restrictions should always be considered.

How To Build A Better Spreadsheet

Recommended columns include product name, category, W2C link, estimated price, QC notes, size details, shipping concerns and final decision.

A good spreadsheet becomes a long-term shopping assistant for tracking research and comparing future purchases.

Advanced Buyer Tips

Experienced buyers record previous QC results, compare different product sources and keep notes about sizing and shipping experience.

The goal of a spreadsheet is not simply finding cheaper products. It is creating a repeatable and organized buying process.
