PikobuyChina FAQ for W2C, QC Photos and Shipping Preparation

This independent FAQ gives buyers a cleaner way to understand product routes, QC photo review, spreadsheet organization and shipping preparation before opening final product links.

Quick note: Always verify live platform information, product availability, route rules and international shipping details before making a buying decision.
What is PikobuyChina?

PikobuyChina is an independent product-route hub. It helps organize W2C search intent, product categories, QC photo reminders and shipping preparation notes.

Is PikobuyChina an official Pikobuy site?

No. It is an independent guide and discovery page. It does not replace official platform rules, support messages, seller details or live shipping calculators.

What does W2C mean?

W2C means “where to cop,” or where to find a product. A good W2C route should include product context, category, image, notes and a live destination to verify.

Why should I check QC photos?

QC photos help you review the item before shipment. Look for shape, color, stitching, material, labels, size tags, hardware, packaging and obvious defects.

Can I trust a product only because it appears on a spreadsheet?

No. A spreadsheet is only an organization tool. You should still review live links, product photos, item notes, platform terms and QC images.

How should I compare shipping options?

Check estimated weight, volume, product type, route restrictions and the current shipping calculator. Bulky boxes and special item attributes can change the final cost.

Why are product prices approximate?

Prices can change on live pages. Currency estimates are for planning only, and the final product page should always be treated as the source of truth.

What should I do before approving a parcel?

Review the product page, QC photos, size notes, quantity, packaging, shipping route and any restrictions that may affect delivery.

Can electronics be shipped the same way as clothes?

Not always. Electronics, batteries and special materials may require different shipping routes or additional restrictions. Verify the current rules before shipment.

How does this FAQ help SEO?

It creates a dedicated page for buyer questions around W2C, QC, shipping and product routes, giving search engines a clearer structure beyond the homepage.
