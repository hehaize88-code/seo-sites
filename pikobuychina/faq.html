PikobuyChina FAQ: W2C, QC Photos and Shipping Guide

What is PikobuyChina?

PikobuyChina is an independent guide for organizing product routes, W2C information, QC photo checks and shopping research.

What does W2C mean?

W2C means where to find a product. It helps buyers locate product sources and research details.

Why are QC photos important?

QC photos help buyers review visible product details before shipment.

How should I check QC photos?

Check shape, color, stitching, labels, materials, accessories and packaging.

How many QC photos should I review?

Review enough angles to understand important product details before approving a parcel.

Can I request extra QC photos?

Available inspection options depend on the current platform service rules.

Is video QC available?

Video inspection availability depends on current service options.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping depends on weight, volume, destination, route and parcel conditions.

Can multiple products be combined?

Many buyers organize multiple items together, but shipping rules should always be checked.

Why does shipping price change?

Weight updates, packaging changes and route differences can affect final costs.

What should beginners do first?

Start with product research, W2C checking, QC review and shipping planning.

Does a spreadsheet guarantee product quality?

No. A spreadsheet is an organization tool, not a replacement for inspection.

How do I compare products?

Compare source details, images, notes, QC information and final costs.

What items need extra attention?

Electronics, bulky products and special materials may require additional checks.

Why use a structured buying workflow?

A workflow helps reduce mistakes and improves purchase decisions.

Is PikobuyChina an official platform?

No. It is an independent information and route guide.

Do prices stay fixed?

Prices may change on live product pages and should be verified.

What is the best way to save finds?

Record product names, links, QC notes and decisions in your spreadsheet.

Can advanced buyers use spreadsheets?

Yes. Experienced buyers use structured notes to manage repeated research.
