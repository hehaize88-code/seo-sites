Pikobuy Spreadsheet 2026 Guide

A useful Pikobuy spreadsheet should do more than collect random links. Buyers need product names, first images, categories, source platforms, estimated USD prices, QC notes and a clean path to product details. This structure matches search intent around Pikobuy spreadsheet, W2C links, China shopping links and product finds.

Before ordering, compare item photos, seller options, size notes, estimated weight and possible shipping restrictions. Final landed cost still depends on stock, domestic shipping, international route, actual weight, volumetric weight and coupon availability.

How to Check QC Photos

QC photos help buyers check visible details before international shipping. Shoes require toe shape, heel tab and pair balance. Clothing requires print scale, stitching, collar and measurements. Bags require pattern alignment, corners, strap, hardware and lining checks.

Extra detailed photos may be useful when a key detail is not visible. Video QC availability and exact fees should be confirmed inside the order dashboard or with customer service.

Shipping Cost and Coupons

International shipping is not fixed. Destination, product type, weight, volume, parcel size, route and packaging choices all affect the final quote. Public Pikobuy terms mention a platform service fee calculated as 8% of international freight.

Coupons may include shipping discounts, referral rewards or campaign credits. Because promotions change, pages should avoid fixed coupon promises unless the current dashboard confirms them.

How many free QC photos are included?

Public pages confirm warehouse photos, but a fixed free-photo count should be checked in the order dashboard.

How much is an extra photo?

Additional detailed photos may be available, but the current price should be confirmed before purchase.

Is video QC available?

Do not assume a fixed video QC option. Check the order page or customer service for availability and price.

What does W2C mean?

W2C means “where to cop”, usually the source link buyers use before ordering through an agent.
