Confirmed public rules

Use cautious rule wording on HipoBuys.de

Hipobuy is publicly presented as a China shopping agent. Publicly unconfirmed numbers should not be written as fixed promises; visitors should verify live shipping quotes, coupon status, exchange rates and value-added service prices inside the active account or order page.

ShippingExplain freight as destination, route, item type, actual weight, volumetric weight and packaging dependent. Do not promise one fixed Germany rate.
QC photosTeach buyers what to inspect, but do not claim a fixed free photo count unless a current public platform page confirms it.
Extra photos / videoAsk users to confirm current availability and price in the order or warehouse interface.
Coupons / exchange rateUse flexible wording because coupons, payment screens and currency conversion can change by account, campaign and time.
